Mashed Potatoes

Enjoy these delicious mashed potatoes with gravy and rolls!

These are the BEST buttery and fluffy tots you'll ever eat!

Great for kids, neighbors, and yourself.

Ingredients:

  • 5 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes | Peeled and cut into 2 inch chunks
  • 8 cups Water
  • Kosher Salt
  • 1.5 lbs Butter
  • 1/4 cup of Milk / Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a large pot. Add so much salt that the water taste like sea water.
  2. Once boiling, add potatoes and boil for 20 minutes or until nearly falling apart.
  3. Drain potatoes and mash with the rest of the ingredients
