Hummus

Enjoy this delicious Hummus with pita bread!

A wonderful smooth and creamy dip.

Great for kids, neighbors, and yourself.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Chickpeas
  • 1/8 cup of Tahina
  • Kosher Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Herbs and Spices
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients together until smooth.
