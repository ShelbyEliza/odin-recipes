Pizza

Enjoy this delicious Pizza with ranch or marinara!

Gooey cheese and crisp crust!

Great for kids, neighbors, and yourself.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 2 cups of cheese | Cheddar and Mozzarella Mix
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Work the dough into a thin round disk.
  2. Cover with sauce. Leave 1/2 inch bare around the perimeter.
  3. Spread cheese over sauce.
  4. Bake on the oven highest setting for 10 minutes.
